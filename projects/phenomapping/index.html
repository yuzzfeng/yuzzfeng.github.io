PhenoMapping: Curation and Verification of Historical Phenological Data

PhenoMapping is a participatory platform designed to help historians, scientists, and volunteers curate and verify historical phenological records. The project supports transparent data cleaning, georeferencing, and quality checks, so long-term observations can be reused for Earth system and climate research.

Funding

Funded by the NFDI4Earth Incubator Lab in collaboration with the Bavarian State Archives (GDA).

PI: Dr. Lina Horl and Dr. Yu Feng
