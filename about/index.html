About

I am Professor of Applied Geoinformatics at Hochschule Mainz - University of Applied Sciences. From 2022 to 2025 I was a Lecturer / Postdoctoral Researcher in Geoinformatics and Cartography at the Technical University of Munich (TUM), Chair of Cartography and Visual Analytics, under the lead of Prof. Dr.-Ing. Liqiu Meng. Prior to 2022, I worked as a research associate at the Institute of Cartography and Geoinformatics (ikg), Leibniz University Hannover. I completed my PhD with distinction (summa cum laude) in 2021 under the supervision of Prof. Dr.-Ing. habil. Monika Sester.

My research interests include

  • Geospatial artificial intelligence (GeoAI) and question answering
  • Volunteered Geographic Information (VGI) for disaster monitoring
  • Cartographic generalization with deep learning methods
  • Mobile Laser Scanning (MLS) data processing and modeling

Selected projects and grants

  • DAAD PPP Projects: a real-world data-driven simulator for interaction-aware autonomous driving benchmarking.
  • NFDI4Earth Incubator: Prompt-accessed GeoQAmap for Earth Data (PI, 2023–2024).
  • ISPRS initiatives: BeBaOI — benchmark and baseline methods for determining overlapping images.
  • TUM Global Incentive Fund: knowledge discovery and visual analysis for urban mobility data (co-PI, 2023–2024).
  • NFDI4Earth Educational Pilots: Coding4Geo (PI, 2022–2023).

If you are interested in research, collaborations or thesis supervision, please contact me at y.feng@tum.de.
