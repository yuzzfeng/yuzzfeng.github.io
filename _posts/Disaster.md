# VGI Applications for Disaster Events

### Beijing Rainstorm on July 21-22, 2012

Heavy storm leads to death and huge loss, analysis are based on Sina Weibo. 20-h period, 170mm (City) - 215mm (Town), 86% of city > 100mm, 

#### Observation:

*Wang, K., Wang, L., Wei, Y. M., & Ye, M. (2013). Beijing storm of July 21, 2012: observations and reflections. Natural hazards, 67(2), 969-974.*

#### Application:

LDA applied to extract topics, 

*Wang, Y., Wang, T., Ye, X., Zhu, J., & Lee, J. (2015). Using social media for emergency response and urban sustainability: A case study of the 2012 Beijing rainstorm. Sustainability, 8(1), 25.*

*Zhai, W., & Thill, J. C. (2017, November). Social Media Discourse in Disaster Situations: A Study of the Deadly July 21, 2012 Beijing Rainstorm. In Proceedings of the 3rd ACM SIGSPATIAL Workshop on Emergency Management using (p. 3). ACM.*
