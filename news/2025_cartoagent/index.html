CartoAgent: a multimodal large language model-powered multi-agent cartographic framework for map style transfer and evaluation

A multimodal large language model-powered multi-agent framework that automates three key stages of cartographic practice—preparation, map design, and evaluation—for map style transfer and evaluation. It coordinates specialized LLM-based agents (e.g., image appreciator, stylesheet designer, map reviewer) to generate maps that are both visually appealing and geographically accurate. Extensive experiments and a human evaluation study show that CartoAgent can generalize to diverse styles while keeping vector data intact.

Paper: CartoAgent (Taylor & Francis)
